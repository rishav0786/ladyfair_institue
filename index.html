Ladyfair Trainning Center

The Ladyfair District Training Center Program aims to establish 1 center in each district of India (maximum 2 in special cases) by partnering with existing beauty parlours and local entrepreneurs. Through this partnership, you will offer certified training, beauty services, and generate income under the Ladyfair brand.

  • Certified Beauty Training Courses.
  • Structured, skill-based programs with Ladyfair certification.
  • High-quality services for clients under Ladyfair standards.
  • Earn through training fees, service bookings, and product sales.

This program enables local businesses to grow, create employment opportunities for women, and ensures standardized beauty services

Which courses are offered at Ladyfair Institute?

We offer a wide range of certified training programs in beauty and wellness, including:

  • Basic & Advanced Beautician Course
  • Hair Artistry
  • Makeup & Bridal Artistry
  • Mehandi & Saree Draping
  • Skin & Facial Treatments
  • Personal Grooming
  • Salon Management
  • Spa & Wellness Therapies

    • What is the eligibility criteria for enrolling in a course at Ladyfair Institute?

    Eligibility criteria varies depending on the course. Generally, candidates must have completed at least their 10thgrade education. Some advanced courses may require prior experience or qualifications in the beauty and wellness industry.

    How can I apply for a course at Ladyfair Institute?

    To apply, simply visit our website and complete the enquiry form. Our expert career counsellor will contact you shortly based on your submitted details.

    What is the duration of the courses at Ladyfair Institute?

    The duration of each course depends on its type. Short-term programs may span a few weeks, while in-depth diploma courses can take several months. Degree programs typically require three years to complete. For exact durations, please refer to the specific course details on our website.

    Are the courses certified and recognized at Ladyfair Institute?

    Yes, all Ladyfair courses come with professional certification, recognized across salons and beauty institutions.

    Does Ladyfair Institute provide job placement assistance?

    Yes, we offer placement assistance to qualified students through our network of partner salons and the Ladyfair platform.

    Are online courses available at Ladyfair Institute?

    Yes, we provide select online training modules with live trainer support and certification.

    How can I contact Ladyfair Institute for more information?

    You can contact us via phone, email, or by visiting our school. Our contact details are available on the 'Contact Us' page of our website - https://www.ladyfair.in .
